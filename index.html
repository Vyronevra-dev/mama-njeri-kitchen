OUR TOP MENU

STARTERS

Succulent, chilled prawns served in a tangy, creamy cocktail sauce, garnished with fresh herbs and lemon slices.

Crab Cakes

Golden-brown, crispy patties filled with flaky, sweet crab meat and minimal filler, served with a signature sauce like saffron or champagne aioli.

MAINS

Beef Fillet with Truffle Butter & Root Mash

A premium, tender cut of pan-seared beef fillet topped with aromatic truffle-infused butter. Served with a velvety potato or pumpkin mash and seasonal root vegetables.

Kuku Paka (Coconut Chicken Curry)

Charcoal-roasted chicken pieces simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce of coconut milk, turmeric, ginger, and green chilies. Served with fluffy rice or soft chapatis.

Grilled Tilapia with Kachumbari & Ugali

Fresh lake tilapia grilled over open flame (flame-charred) to bring out natural smoky flavors. Served with a side of kachumbari and traditional ugali.

ABOUT US

Established in early 2022 by a passionate local chef, Mama Njeri. The kitchen began as a small family-run catering business before evolving into one of the city's most beloved luxurious dining destinations. The restaurant was founded on the philosophy of "elevated authenticity," aiming to bridge the gap between traditional Kenyan home cooking and modern fine-dining trends. By 2026, it has become famous for its meticulous sourcing of local ingredients—like fresh Lake Victoria tilapia and premium Kenyan beef—which are used to create the award-winning dishes that have come to define its menu.

The dining experience at Mama Njeri's begins with their signature Classic Bruschetta and succulent Prawn Cocktail, setting a high standard for the meal ahead. Guests often transition to the restaurant's crown jewel. Their Foods provides a modern, decadent end to a meal that honors both Kenya's rich heritage and the global flavors of today.
